---------------------------------

Activating your **JCPenney Credit Card** is quick and easy. Just follow the steps below to get started and enjoy shopping benefits right away.

Steps to Activate Your JCPenney Credit Card
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

1. Visit the official activation page at:
